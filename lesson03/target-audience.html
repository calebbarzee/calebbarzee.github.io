Target Audience:

The target audience for Sonorous Forecasts are presumptuous people aged 20-60 years old. They are those people that are "too good" for conventional weather sites. They are individuals that not only care what information is presented to them, but also how that information is presented.

Personas:

Janessa

Janessa frequently flies from Paris, France to Florence, Italy to purchase textiles for her designer clothing brand. She wants an elegant way to know what the weather is going to be like in the city she's arriving.

Edward

Edward likes to frequently sun bath at his villa in the Virgin Islands. However, he doesn't wish to go through the hassle of ordering his staff to prepare his private jet unless, there's a forecast of at least four days of uninterrupted sun.
