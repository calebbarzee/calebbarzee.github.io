Headings:

I chose the font Imbue for the headings on the webpages. I chose this font because it is a serif font. I wanted to have the headings appear nice and fancy.

Paragraphs:

I chose the font Armio for the paragraphs on the webpages. I chose this font because it is a san-serif font and thus easily readable. I also wanted this font to just appear clean and semi-modern.
