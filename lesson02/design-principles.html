Design Principles

Rule of Thirds

SpaceX

SpaceX is a space exploration company based in the United States. Their website uses the rule of thirds by placing powerful imagery on the upper right rule of thirds crosshair, and then a bold description in the lower left crosshair.

White Space

FModern

FModern is a Japanese furniture company. Their website does an excellent job at using whitespace. This really puts a spotlight on their products as well as making the user unencumbered and free.

PARC: Alignment

Tesla

Tesla is an electronics company that primarily manufactures batteries, electric vehicles, and solar cells. The website uses really beautiful alignment. Even in a narrow viewport, the text is spaced evenly, centered, and everything is aligned on an imaginary grid.
